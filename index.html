You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Plastics bellow. Clicking a Plasting casts her lunch vote and will add a specific amount of votes to your total score.

You can sit with us if you match your total score to the random number, you sit with the Art Freaks if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each plastics vote is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the voting begins, the game will change the values of each Plastic's vote.
